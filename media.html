You must be wondering, Who’s Dave?

Dave, whose full name is David Orobosa Omoregie, is a British rapper, singer, songwriter, and record producer. Born on June 5, 1998, in Streatham, London, Dave quickly rose to prominence in the UK music scene with his thought-provoking lyrics, introspective themes, and versatile musical style. His debut album, "Psychodrama," released in 2019, received critical acclaim and won the Mercury Prize, establishing him as one of the most influential voices in contemporary British rap. Dave's music often explores themes of identity, mental health, and social issues, resonating deeply with his audience.
